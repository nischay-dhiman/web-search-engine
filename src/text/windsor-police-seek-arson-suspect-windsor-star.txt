Windsor police seek arson suspect Back to video Firefighters and patrol officers responded to the scene shortly after 3 a.m. No one was hurt, but the home — a two-story residence — was extensively damaged by the fire. Four occupants of the residence were displaced by the incident. Investigators examined the premises and deemed the fire to be suspicious. Further investigation pointed to an individual who was recorded on a surveillance camera shortly before the fire — between 2 a.m. and 3 a.m. on Sept. 30. The house in the 700 block of Windsor Avenue following an early morning fire in Windsor on Sept. 30, 2021. Photo by Dax Melmer /Windsor Star Windsor police describe the suspect as a white female who was carrying several bags and a black backpack. At the time of the recording, she was wearing a black spring jacket, grey pants, black sandals, and a grey baseball cap with a black rim. Anyone with information about this individual — or more surveillance camera recordings of the area at the time of the incident — is asked to call investigators at 519-255-6700 ext. 4330. Anonymous tips can be made via Crime Stoppers at 519-258-8477 or www.catchcrooks.com .
