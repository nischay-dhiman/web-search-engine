Alymer Avenue fire deemed suspicious Back to video Officers attended the area and alerted dispatch to send Windsor Fire and Rescue Services. In the 400 block of Alymer Avenue, police found a shed engulfed in flames. As the fire spread towards a duplex, officers evacuated both homes. No injuries were reported. Windsor Fire crews extinguished the blaze and estimated damages were valued at $125,000. Investigators determined the fire was suspicious and held the scene for the arson unit. Anyone in the area with surveillance cameras is asked to check their footage for possible evidence. Those with information can contact police at 519-255-6700 ext. 4330, or Crime Stoppers at 519-258-8477 (TIPS) or catchcrooks.com . This is the second suspicious fire investigation launched in Windsor in recent days.
