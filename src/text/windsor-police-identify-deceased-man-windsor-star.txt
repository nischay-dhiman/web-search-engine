Windsor police identify deceased man Back to video The Major Crime Unit was then able to confirm the identity of the individual with his immediate family. The death of the man — a 66-year-old Windsor resident — is not considered suspicious. Described as a Middle Eastern man with a slender build and a neatly-trimmed beard, the individual was found in a state of medical emergency on Oct. 25 around 5 a.m. in the area of Dougall Avenue. He passed away a short time later in hospital. He was carrying no identification at the time of his death — leading police to turn to the community for tips. Windsor Police Service thanked the public and the media for their assistance in this matter.
